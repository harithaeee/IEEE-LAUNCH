IEEE LaunchPad Kerala

Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala is a premier job fair organized by the IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. It's a collaborative effort focused on bridging the gap between talented engineering graduates and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This year's event promises to be bigger and better than ever before, offering a unique platform for students, graduates, and companies to connect and propel their career journeys.

Call to Action

Are you an engineering student or graduate seeking career opportunities? Join LaunchPad Kerala 2024! Connect with top companies from diverse sectors, showcase your skills and knowledge, and land your dream job. Companies can also register to access a pool of pre-vetted, top-tier talent aligned with their specific needs.

Latest Updates

LaunchPad Kerala 2024 registration is now open! Don't miss this chance to connect with potential employers or recruit top talent for your company. Visit the official website (https://launchpadkerala.org/) to register today!

Event Roadmap

  • **Job Fair:** Network with leading companies from various sectors like IT, aerospace, electronics, and more. Explore exciting career opportunities and get a head start on your job search.
  • **Pre-event workshops:** Enhance your skills and prepare for the job fair with targeted workshops on resume writing, interview techniques, and industry trends led by experienced professionals.
  • **Unique Assessment Process:** Showcase your abilities through a blend of online tests and a rigorous technical challenge, allowing companies to identify the best fit for their roles.
  • **Flexible Interview Locations:** Shortlisted candidates can choose their preferred interview location in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, streamlining the process for both companies and candidates.
